Goal

Our goal is to provide you with a complete stack of tools to develop apps, unifying:

  • A code generator based on boilerplates
  • A fast & secure app runtime
  • A multi-platform build matrix
  • A cloud-based build service
  • A CLI (Command Line Interface) and WebUI
